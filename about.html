ABOUT ME

My name is Thembelihle Ngobese. I am a 23 year old female from Gauteng, Johannesburg in South Africa. I am a fullstack web developer and this is my first website.

As a child growing up, I used to watch movies with a lot of hacking, programming and I didn't even know the word but it was really interesting to watch. I knew right then that it is what I'd like to pursue in future. In 2019, I had an opportunity to do a certificate in computer literacy and that grew my interest to know more about computers, specifically the software side of computers. In 2020, I enrolled at a college for Information Technology and Computer Science level 2 which was a vocational course. The course was paid for by my mother, she got retrenched and I couldn't continue with level 3 and 4. Late 2022, I came across an ALX ad on facebook and I clicked on the link to learn more about it. This year January, I took a leap of faith and applied for the fulltime software engineering course offered by ALX. The first few monnths were a lot of fun, I made friends and I learned a lot of things, some I did not even know existed. I will forever be grateful for the opportunity even though I couldn't finish the programme.
